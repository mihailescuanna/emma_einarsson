Hej!

I'm Emma, a 26 years old lover of people and life. Born and raised in Reykjavik, I've dedicated my whole life to my biggest passion and the thing I love most: photography. I love capturing the beautiful moments life has to offer to us and share my energy to other people.

My main power stands in portrait, product and events photography. I've worked in photography studios such as Rakel Rune, Lundi Creative Studio and North Pole Studio in the present. I've participated as a photographer in a variety of events, such as: Reykjavik Festival City, Braedslan Music Festival, Aldrei For Eg Sudur, Bergenfest Trondheim, Ilosaarirock Joensuu and many more.
